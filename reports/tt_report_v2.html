TL;DR

The TikTok skill (av-tiktok-twin) ran end-to-end on 10 real creators — finance, nutrition, medicine, skincare, wellness, plus a Romanian-language and an AI-generated source — with zero new code. Every twin trained, every voice cloned, every arc rendered. Three findings define the deliverable.

caption-free generated looks vs caption-bleeding raw twins
Three findings. (1) Caption bleed. Avatar V is cross-reference-driven, so a raw twin from caption-heavy short-form footage faithfully reproduces the burned-in captions. The Image-N generated-look path is caption-free — making it the recommended deliverable for social creators. (2) Dual-self sources break raw twins. Vivian Tu's viral “Regular vs Rich” format films two of her on screen at once; the raw twin reproduces both figures. The look path sidesteps it. (3) The twin window must be a pure talking-head — short-form interleaves graphics, b-roll and reaction-PIP; a contact sheet catches it in seconds (cameron's first window failed "missing faces").

What we learned (TikTok)

  • The platform layer is a non-issue. yt-dlp pulls and even enumerates TikTok cleanly; the twin/voice/looks/render stack is identical across YouTube, IG, and TikTok.
  • Twin window selection is the #1 operational risk. Short-form creators interleave talking-head with graphics, b-roll, and title-cards — the window must be a pure, continuous talking segment or training fails "missing faces." A contact sheet catches this in seconds.
  • Look-gen identity is prompt-sensitive and recoverable. Across 8 creators it ranged 0.59 → 0.85 after re-rolls. A first pass can collapse (cameron 0.14); an explicit identity clause ("same face, wearing glasses") recovered it to 0.81. The twin succeeding doesn't guarantee the looks — but a targeted re-roll fixes them.
  • Caption bleed is the headline finding. Raw Avatar V twins reproduce burned-in source captions (and dual-self compositions); the Image-N generated-look path is caption-free and is the recommended deliverable for short-form creators.
  • Language is handled. Romanian (aripa) and Portuguese (Paulo, IG) cloned + rendered fine via ElevenLabs v3.

12Method

Per account: yt-dlp pulls the TikTok → pick a clean continuous talking-head window from a contact sheet → extract a native-9:16 twin clip + voice sample → create an Avatar V digital twin via /v2/video_avatar with a pre-cleared R&D consent video bundled → clone the voice → generate two Image-N looks (Seedance, ArcFace-scored) → render a 4-beat emotion arc (ElevenLabs v3) on the real look and a generated look → host + report. Internal HeyGen R&D on the operator's own account.

Skill: av-tiktok-twin (adapted from av-emotional-twin). Identity = ArcFace cosine vs a source reference frame.
